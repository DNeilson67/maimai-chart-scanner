How the reveal works

maimai only ever shows you a whole-number-ish level like 13 or 13+ in the arcade. Behind the scenes every chart also has an internal level — a precise decimal value like 13.4 — used for scoring (DX Rating) and generally agreed on by the community rather than published by SEGA. This tool matches what your camera sees against a community-maintained chart database and shows that hidden decimal next to the level you already know.

Song, sheet and internal-level data comes from arcade-songs.zetaraku.dev (data fetched live, client-side, from its public dataset). Internal levels are community-sourced and occasionally revised — treat them as very well-informed estimates, not official numbers. This project is unofficial and not affiliated with SEGA.

Camera OCR works best on the in-game song/difficulty select screen where the title and level are both clearly visible on screen — plain jacket art alone often won't have readable text. If recognition misses, use manual search instead.
